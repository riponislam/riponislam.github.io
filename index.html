CCN UNUVERSITY
OF SCIENCE &
TECHNOLOGY

Comilla is the ancient locality. From the old period it is a region of advanced people. There the glory of Natural bounties and beauties is added with that of pristine heritage . Considering several facts, the habitation of this area is enriched.

Kotbari in Comilla is the entertainment center of civilization. Nearly 1400 hundred years ago, there built up many residential institutes from which Shalban Bihar is the exceptional one. Here search for knowledge and its cultivation have already been done. Half and one thousand year ago, one of the world’s best Universities that is Nalanda and its great scholar and chancellor named Shilvadra was the son of Kailan in West Chandina. Still Kotbari is familiar as an education center of Comilla. Various colleges and Universities are set up here. And following this line, CCN University of Science & Technology is the last addition.

According to the approved of Peoples Republic of Bangladeshi honorable prime minister and the permission of education ministry it was established in 2014. After that , the Grant Commission of University permitted the admission and teaching approaches comprised of & programmers in 18 May 2015.

In this context, UGC approved BBA, MBA, LLB, LLM, Bangla, English, Economics and Mathematics programmers admit the learners from September 2015: session and launches its academic activities. Facilitative learning with low-expenditure is also present in this campus.

The green and peaceful environment is framed in a pair. The campus surrounded by hills becomes well-sounding place through the uproars of students. Certainly CCN University of Science & Technology is the remarkable institute of the education journey of 21st century.

The ex-ministers of law, Justice and Parliament the presidium member of Awami League and MP named Abdul Matin Khasru is the President of Board of Trusties of this University. In this board , there are some education experts and specialists. Hens, the Board of Trusties are registered by the joint stock company.

Keeping its connection with the Act of Non-Govt. University, the University’s Syndicate, Academic Council, Finance committee, Curriculum committee, Teachers’ circular committee, Discipline committee, Charge committee, Purchase committee, Admission committee, Education improvement committee etc. are working.

With the grant by the president of people’s Republic of Bangladesh and Chancellor of Universities, Dr. Md. Mosharaf Hossen joins as a Vice chancellor and skillfully handles its activities.

With the decision of Board of Trusties, the ex- principle of Comilla Victoria College Dr. A. K. M. Assaduzzaman is playing the role of Pro-vice chancellor.

With permission given by the president of Peoples Republic of Bangladesh and the Chancellor other Universities Dr. Ali Hossen Chaudhury has joined as University Treasurer.

According to the decision of Board of Trusties , Mr. Md. Tariqul Islam Chaudhury is playing the responsibility of registrar . he is the secretary of Board of Trusties as well as the founder of this campus.

With the ownership of land and self-expenditure here different faculties are set up that is located in Kotbari, an educated center surrounded by hills, and natural sceneries. In the meantime, Front office, Library, Academic building, Administrative building, Research center, etc. are set up. According to the necessities, there will also be other faculties by the decision of Board of Trusties.

Prof. Dr. Md. Musharaf Hossain
Vice Chancellor, CCN UST

CCN University of "Scince & Technology Started it's function of academic activities in 2016. At that time there was no such appointed Vice Chancellor. It Last June, 2017 Honorable President Abdul Hamid, Chancellor of the University has appointed Professor Dr. Mosharaf Hossain as Vice Chancellor of CCN University of Science & Technology for four years. He was working as Dean, Faculty of Business Administration of Khustia Islamic University, Bangladesh. Before that he had worked as Dean, Faculty of Business Administration of some private Universities in Bangladesh. Last 9 June, 2017 he joined here at CCN University of Science & Technology and now he is working with great care and love as well as with more sincerity.
