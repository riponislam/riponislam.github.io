Welcome to CCN University of Science & Technology, an UGC & Govt. approved Private University in Comilla Bangladesh, which is a worldwide University in the soil of Comilla established in 2015. It’s visioning to provide the best and quality education to make our country well recognized in the field of Science and Technology. It also aims to make our new generation more skilled and experienced by providing practical education.

Professor Dr. Mahbubul Haque, a famous Linguist, Educationist, Researcher and Writer, was working as the honorable Vice Chancellor (Current Charge) of this University. Recently Prof. Dr. Md. Musharaf Hossain has joined as the Vice Chancellor by being selected by the Honorable Chancellor of the university and Honorable President of The People’s Republic of Bangladesh Adv. Md. Abdul Hamid.

We are one of the Bangladesh’s young private universities with excellent teaching and a growing reputation for our environment of higher education. With our outstanding facilities and strong student focus, we are rapidly becoming one of the most desirable centers for excellence in studying in Bangladesh. Our vision is, and we are committed, to become a world class university and everything we do is directed towards that goal.

It started functioning with six departments. These departments are under the three faculties namely Faculty of Arts & Social Science, Faculty of Law, and Faculty of Business Administration. The departments are-

  • Department of English,
  • Department of Mathematics
  • Department of Economics
  • Department of Law
  • Department of Bangla,
  • Department of Business Administration.
    •

In addition, the university is offering

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Law (LLM).

We are expected to start our engineering faculty very soon. Moreover, the teachers here are highly trained and experienced in different fields. They are from Dhaka University, Chittagong University, Comilla University etc. With the targeted goal they are trying best to make this university a better place of higher education in Bangladesh.
