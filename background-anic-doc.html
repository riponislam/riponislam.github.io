Background of the University


The CCN University of Science and Technology (CCN UST) was founded by the Alhaj Nurul Islam Chowdhury (ANIC) Shiksha Foundation in 2014.

ANIC Shiksha Foundation is a non-profit and non-political voluntary organization in Bangladesh.
Alhaj Nurul Islam Chowdhury was a primary school teacher. Despite living in rural areas, he educated his children in technical and vocational education. As a result, everyone today is established in their respective fields.

Tariqul Islam Chowdhury, son of Mr. Nurul Islam Chowdhury, started the foundation in 2001 to spread the technical and vocational education at the grass root level.
